Thanatology

Thanatology is the desktop application which is exploiting the power of the exhume toolsuite to perform digital forensics and visualize data. This project is currently in its development phase.

Exhume toolsuite

A collection of utilities designed to help forensic investigators and analysts inspect, extract, and interpret data from diverse data sources. One of the core libraries of the Thanatology application.

VolWeb

VolWeb is a digital forensic memory analysis platform that leverages the power of the Volatility 3 framework. It is dedicated to aiding in investigations and incident responses.
